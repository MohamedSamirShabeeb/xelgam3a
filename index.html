Market Context

The Egyptian Academic-to-Career Gap

❌ The Core Problem

Rigid career paths, traditional family biases, and lack of exposure leave high school and university students confused about actual industry demands. Furthermore, advanced postgrads study in isolation without commercial alignment.

💡 The XelGam3a Solution

An on-campus, open-air interactive masterclass that records dynamic podcast sessions showing actual pathways, hosted directly on the student's campus turf to drive peer validation.

Production Strategy

The "Batch Shooting Residency" Model

Producing 72 separate, weekly, high-quality on-campus episodes is logistically impossible. To resolve this, **XelGam3a** operates a streamlined **Residency Model**:

The 4-Day Campus Footprint

  • Days 1 & 2: Capture Tier 1 (8 episodes recorded)
  • Day 3: Capture Tier 2 (8 episodes recorded)
  • Day 4: Capture Tier 3 (8 episodes recorded)

This captures 24 total episodes (one-third of the entire season) in just 4 days. We only need **3 multi-day campus residencies** in total for the entire year!

Technical Footprint Specifications

Set Design
